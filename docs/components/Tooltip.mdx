# Tooltip

Simple tooltip component that shows on hover, click or focus.

## Usage

#### Default

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)">Hover me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Position: ***top***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" position="top">Hover me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Position: ***bottom***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" position="bottom">Hover me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Position: ***left***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" position="left">Hover me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Position: ***right***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" position="right">Hover me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Trigger: ***mouseenter***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" trigger="mouseenter">Hover me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Trigger: ***click***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" trigger="click">Click me!</Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Trigger: ***focus***

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" trigger="focus"><span tabindex="-1">Focus me!</span></Tooltip>
</Playground>

#### Delay

<Playground>
  <Tooltip content="I'm a Tooltip :)" delay="1000">I'm slow!</Tooltip>
</Playground>
